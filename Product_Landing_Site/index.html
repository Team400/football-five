Team 400's: FantasyMax

What is Our Product?

FantasyMax is an open source software product that will allow users to manage a college football fantasy league on an easy-to-use application.

Who is This Product Good For?

This application is designed for avid sports fans who want more fantasy football options than just NFL. It is designed for people who have an interest in developing and maintaining a Fantasy league with friends, co-workers, peers, and family!

Our Slogan

This is YOUR Fantasy, To the MAX.

Our Product Vision

For avid college football fanatics who want a competitive experience with friends through the fantasy sports realm. The FantasyMax App is a web-based gaming service that allows users the same luxuries of fantasy NFL but on the college level, along with various custom options available. Unlike other fantasy sports applications, our project allows users to enjoy the fantasy football experience on the college level while being customizable from draft selection to text formatting on each user's screen.

Our Product's Features

  • Users can choose to have their draft picks automated; some users may not want to hand pick all their players but would rather have the app do this for them.
  • Users can print their picks; users can peruse their selections to get a feel for their roster.
  • Users can select the types of roster selection for their league such as: salary cap leagues, keeper leagues, dynasty leagues, etc... Other fantasy football platforms do not provide this option. Other types of fantasy football apps are tailor made for a particular type of roster selection.
  • Users also have the ability to select the format of the league competition such as: head-to-head league, total points league. Once again other apps don't provide this flexibility, some apps offer traditional league format and others keeper/dynasty league format. For example, the apps that provide a betting pool only operate on daily fantasy sport league format.
  • Users will be able to select a picture to represent their team, the ability to select logo to represent the team gives the user a sense of individuality as if this is the actual logo for their sports organization.

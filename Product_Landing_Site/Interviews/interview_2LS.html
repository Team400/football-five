Interviewer: Larry Schoch

Interviewee: Karen

Tell me about yourself.

  • 60 years old
  • Grew up on a dairy farm near Salem, IN
  • Moved to Madison, IN shortly after graduating from college in her early twenties.
  • Husband - car dealer.
  • Four children and four grandchildren.
  • Boating, hiking, travel.
  • Frequent activities with a small group of close-knit friends.

What is your educational background?

  • BA in English, Hanover College, Hanover, IN
  • Masters in Education, Hanover College, Hanover, IN

Tell me about your work.

  • High school English teacher since earning a masters degree in education at age 23.
  • Same position her entire career and for most of that time she has taught senior English.
  • Takes pride in preparing her students for a successful college experience.
  • Her students know that they will be challenged in her classes but for the most part she is highly regarded.
  • Struggling with decision whether or not to retire and when.

Do you have any interest in a college fantasy football app?

  • Passing interest in college football.
  • Interested in the prospect of participation as a group activity.
  • Imagined various scenarios such as get-togethers with friends to watch the games, group competition, recent losers responsible for providing dinner, snacks, beverages, etc.
  • Wanted to know how her team would be created and showed interest in the draft process.
  • Requested a summary of information about the teams and players to assist her with the draft.

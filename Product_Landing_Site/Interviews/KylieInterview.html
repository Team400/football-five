Tell me a little about yourself

  • Works as a full-time engineer as well as owns a collegiate t-shirt company.
  • Alumna of the University of Louisville & enjoys college football
  • Loves fantasy football, has leagues with friends and family (husband plays as well).


After discussing proposed features that were already thought of, what are some features you would include?

  • As a small business owner, ensuring that all parts of the app (text, font size & style, etc.) is ADA compliant.
  • To promote the idea of diversifying teams, an algorithm (or arbitrary number based on the athletic program's value) should be assigned to the player within the salary cap league.
  • A rating system should be in place based on strength of schedule, team record, opponents, and personal statistics for each player for selection.
  • The size of the leagues should be up to the commissioner (no minimum nor maximum)
  • Easily accessible chat option available to users
  • Team name character lengths should be flexible for creativity
  • Customizable interfaces for users (similar to light/dark mode and color choices)
  • More information on players such as pre-game playlists, pre-game meal suggestions, etc.
  • In terms of NIL deals, ensure players have contracts in place

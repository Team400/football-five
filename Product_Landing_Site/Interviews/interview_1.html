Interview Date: 5/28/2022

Interviewer: Ethan Craycroft

Interviewee: Frank

Is this a product you would use?

  • Yes, played NFL fantasy football for years. Would definitely try a college version
  • Big fan of sports overall but especially College Football.
  • Fan of the University of Cincinnati Bearcats.
  • Loves the idea of fantasy football in general, and likes the idea of the college version even more because that is what he prefers to watch.


What are some features you would need to see included in this appplication?

  • Starting with the draft, there should be multiple options available.
  • This could be straight draft, snake draft, a lottery, etc.
  • Draft should be able to be online OR offline.
  • League would need a schedule of matchups.
  • League should list the current standings of all teams.
  • Free agents need to be listed for everyone to see so team managers can add them to their team.
  • Wavier wire is important - lets managers claim players after a week is done.
  • Player stats should be easily accessible, both fantasy and real life.


What else would you like to see implemented in this application?

  • Customizability as far as league matchup styles, team names, etc.
  • Custom point system (set by league commissioner).
  • Multi-team trades.


Do you have any concerns with the idea of our product?

  • College athletes won't be able to participate.
  • High turnover of players - many players only play 3 years of college before leaving for the NFL.
  • Getting/keeping interest of fantasy players with so many college teams and players to know and study.

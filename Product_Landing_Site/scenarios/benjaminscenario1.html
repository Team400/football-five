Tutorial

general instructions for new beginers

Benjamin is well versed in the game of football but hasn't attended a live game or watch a game on television since he attended college as an undergraduate. Having never played fantasy football but an avid gamer, he wants an app that has a low learning curve and easy to grasp. He plans on using the tutorial feature to get a sense of what it is like to participate in a fantasy football league. Upon using the tutorial feature, a perusal through the FAQ is there to answer any other queries about the general rules and guidelines to fantasy football.

He doesn't want his lack of familiarity to fantasy sports to be a hindrance to the potential benefits of this software. An app that is too complicated would definitely be a deterrent, he would like to be able to select from various skill levels and have some of the more technical details be automated.
